**Flight/Immigration Essential**
- [ ] Passport (valid for at least six months beyond your stay)
- [ ] Visa (if required)
- [ ] Travel insurance
- [ ] Flight tickets
- [ ] Power adapter (Indonesia uses Type C and Type F plugs)
- [ ] Portable charger (power bank)
- [ ] Mobile phone and charger
- [ ] Laptop/tablet and charger (if needed)
- [ ] Copies of important documents (stored separately)
- [ ] Travel locks for your luggage
- [ ] Travel pillow and eye mask (for long flights)
- [ ] Credit/debit cards
- [ ] Prescription medications (with a copy of the prescription)
- [ ] First aid kit (band-aids, antiseptic wipes, pain relievers)

**Travel essentials**
- [ ] Insect repellent (to protect against mosquitoes)
- [ ] Reusable water bottle
- [ ] Sunscreen (high SPF)
- [ ] Face masks and hand sanitizer
- [ ] Lightweight, breathable clothing (Indonesia is hot and humid)
- [ ] Comfortable walking shoes or sandals
- [ ] A light jacket or sweater (for cooler evenings or air-conditioned places)
- [ ] Rain jacket or poncho (June to July is part of the rainy season)
- [ ] Travel-sized toiletries
- [ ] A small backpack or daypack
- [ ] Some cash in USD and local currency (Indonesian Rupiah) 

**Optionals**
- [ ] Extra passport photos (for any unforeseen visa or travel document needs)
