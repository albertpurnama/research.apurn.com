### Month 1: Project Initiation and Planning

1. **Project Kickoff (Week 1)**
    - Establish project goals, scope, and objectives.
    - Necessary to align the team’s vision and set clear expectations.
2. **Requirement Gathering and Analysis (Week 2)**
    - Collect detailed requirements from stakeholders.
    - Critical for understanding what needs to be built and ensuring stakeholder needs are met.
3. **Project Plan Development (Week 3)**
    - Develop a detailed project plan including timelines, resources, and budgets.
    - Ensures that there is a structured approach to executing the project.
4. **Team Formation and Roles Assignment (Week 4)**
    - Assemble the project team and assign specific roles and responsibilities.
    - Necessary for efficient team functioning and clear distribution of tasks.

### Month 2: Design and Development

1. **Design Phase (Weeks 5-6)**
    - Create detailed engineering designs and specifications.
    - Critical for visualizing the end product and serves as a blueprint for development.
2. **Prototype Development (Weeks 7-8)**
    - Develop a working prototype based on the designs.
    - Allows for testing the feasibility of the design and making necessary adjustments.

### Month 3: Testing, Evaluation, and Finalization

1. **Testing Phase (Weeks 9-10)**
    - Conduct rigorous testing of the prototype to identify and fix issues.
    - Ensures the reliability and safety of the engineering solution.
2. **Evaluation and Feedback (Week 11)**
    - Evaluate the project outcomes against objectives and gather stakeholder feedback.
    - Provides insights into the project’s success and areas for improvement.
3. **Final Adjustments and Project Closure (Week 12)**
    - Make any final adjustments based on feedback and prepare project documentation.
    - Necessary for officially closing the project and handing over the deliverables.
