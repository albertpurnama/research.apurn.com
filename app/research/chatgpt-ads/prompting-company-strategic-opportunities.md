**SaaS platform that proves ROI for conversational ads** - the "Google Analytics for conversational commerce."

**Core Features:**
1. Track full journey: Ad ‚Üí Conversation ‚Üí Website ‚Üí Purchase
2. Multi-model attribution (first-touch, last-touch, conversational ML)
3. Conversation depth analysis (which patterns lead to conversion?)
4. Cross-platform tracking (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude)
5. ROI dashboard for advertisers

### Why This Is Strategic

**The Problem:**
- Advertisers spend $50K-500K/month on ChatGPT ads
- They have NO IDEA which ads drive revenue
- "I know half my ad spend is wasted, I just don't know which half"

**Your Solution:**
- Track every step from ad impression ‚Üí purchase
- Prove which conversational ads work (and which don't)
- Show exact ROI per campaign, per ad, per conversation pattern

**Market Need:**
- ChatGPT ads = $1B Year 1 ‚Üí assume 10,000 advertisers
- Every advertiser needs attribution
- **$2-10K/month √ó 10,000 advertisers = $20-100M TAM**
